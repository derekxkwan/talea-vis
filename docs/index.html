Model Directions

  • left click and drag mouse to rotate the view
  • right click and drag mouse to scroll the view
    • arrow keys can also scroll the view (active after any mouse click event)
  • scroll up/down to zoom in/out
  • hover mouse pointer over different model parts to show brief details
    • left click on names of model parts in text info panel (or press the "space" key) to view more details in separate window
    • press the "P" key to play (or pause) audio for the corresponding section of the model
